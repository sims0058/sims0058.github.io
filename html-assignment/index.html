About Me

Buket Simsek Baykal

I am a graphic designer. I am also a researcher who is curious about everything about design and has a passion for design. After graduating from college, I worked for a publisher for about 3 years and designed children's books. I made illustrations for children's books. I have worked as a graphic designer in a union for the last 4 years. This experience has given me many other skills. Now, I am different from 4 years ago both as a person and as a profession. I am currently working freelance from time to time. I am also a full time student at Interactive Media at Algonquin College. It's exciting to be a student again after about 10 years. I have a great opportunity to learn new things and improve myself in interactive media, video, web design and more. Besides all this, I am also the mother of a little boy. If I can find time for all this, I like to read books, walk, watch movies, but more often spend time with my son and play games.
